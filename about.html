Hi, my name is Fiona and I want to make my website about the LGBTQ+ youth community because I’m part of it and I think it’s something that should be addressed. I'm not out yet to my parents, and I'm only out to a few people. My parents are homophobic which is upsetting.

I have many friends that are also part of the community and I think that they shouldn’t be scared about coming out. It's ironic because I'm scared of coming out too and I know why they are afraid.

Many people are homophobic and young people care about what others think, especially me. The way people see me is really important to me even though I know it shouldn't be.

This pushed me to create this since both my parents are homophobic which is ironic since 2/3 of their kids are part of the community.
