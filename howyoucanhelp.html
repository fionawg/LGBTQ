You can help the LGBTQ+ youth community by simply supporting them and letting them know that you are there for them. Many are scared of what others will think of them if they attempt to come out.

Another way you can help is to educate yourself. There are many terms that portray the LGBTQ+ and it can help if you make an effort to learn them. Gender is also something that relates to them that may be confusing if you don't research about it.
